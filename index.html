WELCOME MESSAGE (HEADTEACHER)

Welcome to Cheza senior secondary school, a vibrant and inclusive community dedicated to fostering academic excellence, creativity, and character development.

Our talented faculty, staff, and students work together to create a nurturing environment where every individual can grow, learn, and thrive. At Cheza, we're passionate about providing a well-rounded education that prepares students for success in an ever-changing world.

Our curriculum is designed to challenge, engage, and inspire students to become active participants in their educational journey. We offer a diverse range of academic programs, extracurricular activities, and enrichment opportunities that cater to different interests and abilities.

Our school community is built on the principles of respect, empathy, and responsibility. We encourage students to develop essential life skills, such as critical thinking, problem-solving, communication, and collaboration. Our experienced teachers and support staff provide individualized attention and guidance to ensure every student reaches their full potential.

News

Registration

Jan 20, 2024

We are pleased to announce that the registration for the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB) will commence on February 18th, 2024. Please note that only students who have cleared all their school fees dues will be eligible for registration. Additionally, students must organize and prepare the necessary documents for the registration process, including a copy of their birth certificate, previous academic records, and any other relevant identification documents. We urge all students to start preparing these documents in advance to ensure a smooth and efficient registration process. Thank you for your cooperation.
