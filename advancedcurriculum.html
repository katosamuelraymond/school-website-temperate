ADVANCED LEVEL CURRICULUM

We provide a variety of both arts and science. Arts and Science subjects both play vital roles in shaping well-rounded individuals and driving societal progress. Arts subjects foster creativity, critical thinking, and cultural awareness, preparing students for careers in media, education, and design. Science subjects, on the other hand, develop analytical skills, technological proficiency, and scientific literacy, opening doors to careers in engineering, healthcare, and research. Together, these disciplines encourage interdisciplinary learning, promoting innovative solutions and a holistic understanding of complex global issues.
