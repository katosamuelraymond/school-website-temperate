ORDINARY LEVEL CURRICULUM

At Cheza Secondary School, we are committed to providing a high-quality education that meets the needs of our students in today’s ever-changing world. Our new curriculum is designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and a love for learning.
We are proud to have a team of dedicated and highly qualified teachers who are passionate about education and are equipped with the latest teaching strategies and tools. Our educators are committed to nurturing each student’s potential and guiding them towards academic excellence and personal growthIn response to the evolving educational needs and to address the challenges identified in previous assessments, the curriculum has been updated to better equip students with essential skills and knowledge. The new curriculum focuses on decongesting content to allow for deeper understanding and mastery of fundamental subjects. This change aims to enhance literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking skills among learners.
